About QRTHCCAF

Who We Are

Quick Response to the Challenged Care Foundation (QRTHCCAF) is a non-governmental, non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of the less privileged. We are committed to providing essential social services, including free medical care, skills empowerment, orphanage support, free education, and rehabilitation programs.

Since our inception, we have made significant strides in humanitarian services, including successful outreach events in various states in Nigeria, where we provided free medical supplies, checkups, and treatments to those in need.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life for underprivileged individuals and communities through healthcare, education, skills development, and economic empowerment.

Our Vision

To be a leading humanitarian organization that provides sustainable support to vulnerable individuals, creating a society where everyone has access to healthcare, education, and economic opportunities.

Our Key Focus Areas

Healthcare

Providing free medical services and supplies to underserved communities

Education

Offering free education and learning resources to vulnerable children

Orphanage Support

Establishing and managing homes for vulnerable children

Skills Empowerment

Training and developing entrepreneurs for self-reliance
